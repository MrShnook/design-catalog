## Agent Quick Reference

Use this catalog to pick fonts for design work. Key selection criteria:

**For SaaS/Tech products:** General Sans, Space Grotesk, Plus Jakarta Sans, Satoshi, Outfit, Switzer
**For editorial/magazine:** Zodiak, Gambetta, Sentient, Erode, Lora, Literata
**For branding/logos:** Clash Display, Clash Grotesk, Cabinet Grotesk, Panchang, Satoshi
**For display/impact:** Bebas Neue, Tanker, Nippo, Anton, Sharpie, Array
**For script/decorative:** Telma, Britney, Dancing Script, Melodrama
**For code/mono:** JetBrains Mono, Azeret Mono
**For friendly/rounded:** Nunito, Quicksand, Pally, Roundo, Chillax
**Variable fonts:** Satoshi, General Sans, Zodiak, Erode, Manrope, Archivo (look for 'var' in Styles)

All fonts: Free for commercial use (verify license in detail JSON for specifics).
