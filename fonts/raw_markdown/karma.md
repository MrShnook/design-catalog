### Story

Karma was developed for use in body text on screen. The characters feature a construction style that tends toward the monolinear. Karma’s characters are economic in width, and the Latin sports a tall x-height. Joana Correia designed Karma for the Indian Type Foundry, who first published the fonts in 2014.

Karma is licensed under the SIL Open Font License. You can find more details about this project [here](https://github.com/itfoundry/karma).
