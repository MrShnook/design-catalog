In this fast-paced world, it is important to take a moment and reflect on our journey. We often get caught up in the rat race and forget what is truly important in life. We strive for success, wealth, and fame, but these things are fleeting and can never bring lasting happiness. True fulfillment and joy can only be found within ourselves, through self-discovery and the pursuit of our passions. It's important to remember that life is a journey, not a destination. Every moment, every experience, is an opportunity for growth and learning. We must embrace challenges and obstacles as opportunities for growth and transformation. It's easy to get caught up in comparing ourselves to others and measuring our success by external standards, but true success is defined by our own personal growth and happiness. Embrace the present moment and let go of the past and future, for it is in this moment that we have the power to shape our own destiny. Embrace your unique qualities, strengths, and weaknesses and allow them to guide you on your journey. The world needs more individuals who are true to themselves, who live with purpose and passion, and who inspire others to do the same. We are all here for a reason, and it is up to us to discover what that reason is and to live it to the fullest.

### Story

Literata is designed by [TypeTogether](http://www.type-together.com/). It was commissioned for [Google Play Books](https://play.google.com/store/books). Two versions of the family exist, one for print and the other for Ebooks. This is the print version of the family.

Literata is licensed under the SIL Open Font License. You can find more details about this project [here](https://github.com/googlefonts/literata).
