### Story

Public Sans is a strong, neutral typeface for interfaces, text, and headings. Developed by the United States Web Design System. This typeface is based on [Libre Franklin](https://github.com/impallari/Libre-Franklin).

Public Sans is licensed under the SIL Open Font License. You can find more details about this project [here](https://github.com/uswds/public-sans).
