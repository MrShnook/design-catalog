### Story

Rosaline is a casual script face, inspired by brush-lettering. Its letterforms are wide and have high stroke contrast. Being a connected script design, Rosaline’s letters all connect with one another (even the ‘@’ connects, which creates great-looking e-mail address settings). Text set in Rosaline looks as if it had been written quickly, and everything bounces a little bit. The numeral are oldstyle figures, differentiating Rosaline from many other informal scripts. Rosalie is a great fit for shop, cafés, and restaurant identities, as well as for holiday cards and invitations.
